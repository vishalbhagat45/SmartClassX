Admission Process

  1. Visit the official SmartClassX website or campus office.
  2. Fill out the application form online or collect it from the campus.
  3. Submit necessary documents (see below).
  4. Appear for an entrance assessment (if required for IIT/NEET).
  5. Wait for confirmation via SMS/email or check online portal.
  6. Pay the course fee upon selection to secure your seat.
